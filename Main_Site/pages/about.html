About Wyrd Foundation

I am a junior at the University of Montana, studying creative writing, English literature, and graphic design. I really love reading and writing. I mostly write fantasy, but I read fantasy, adventure, young adult, and sci-fi novels. I have two brothers, two parents, and four grandparents.

I chose this project because I made these cupcakes for my boyfriend as a present during our last anniversary. At the time, he couldn't eat chocolate and felt like there were few desserts that were safe for him. I like this recipe because it's different from most other cake-like recipies, kind of like a cross between a cupcake and a danish or jelly donut. Also, other than cooking, I'm only really good at writing and swimming, which are hard to write instructions for.

I got the recipies for the frosting and the filling on these sites.
